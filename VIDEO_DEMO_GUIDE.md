## üìù SCRIPT (60-90 seconds)

### ‚è±Ô∏è Section 1: Hook (0:00-0:10) - 10 seconds

**VISUAL:** Show chaotic logs or alert dashboard

**NARRATION:**
> "When production crashes at 3 AM, DevOps engineers manually dig through thousands of log entries for hours. This incident? It took us 30 seconds."

**KEY POINTS:**
- Start with the pain point
- Use dramatic contrast (hours vs seconds)
- Show urgency

---

### ‚è±Ô∏è Section 2: Problem (0:10-0:20) - 10 seconds

**VISUAL:** Show messy log file or frustrated engineer (stock image)

**NARRATION:**
> "Traditional incident response is slow, manual, and error-prone. Engineers waste 2+ hours per incident, costing hundreds of dollars in lost time."

**KEY POINTS:**
- Quantify the problem (2 hours, $200+)
- Visual should show complexity/chaos
- Keep it brief, don't dwell

---

### ‚è±Ô∏è Section 3: Solution Demo (0:20-0:60) - 40 seconds

This is the CORE of your demo. Show the app working!

#### **Step 1: Upload (0:20-0:25) - 5 seconds**

**VISUAL:** Your app interface, upload logs

**NARRATION:**
> "Our solution: Six AI agents working together. Watch."

**ACTIONS:**
- Show clean UI
- Click "Load Sample Logs"
- Highlight the "6 agents" mentioned

#### **Step 2: Real-Time Analysis (0:25-0:50) - 25 seconds**

**VISUAL:** Live agent progress (YOUR NEW FEATURE!)

**NARRATION:**
> "The Log Reader agent parses entries... the Remediation agent finds solutions using RAG... RCA agent performs root cause analysis... Notification and JIRA agents alert the team... and the Cookbook agent creates a playbook. All in 30 seconds."

**ACTIONS:**
- Click "Analyze Incident"
- **CRITICAL:** Show agents updating in real-time
- Show progress bar moving
- Pause briefly on each agent (2-3 seconds each)
- Show agent cards changing: Pending ‚Üí Processing ‚Üí Completed

#### **Step 3: Results (0:50-0:60) - 10 seconds**

**VISUAL:** Switch to Analysis tab, show business metrics

**NARRATION:**
> "Results: 5 issues found, remediation plans generated, JIRA tickets created. Time saved: 1.9 hours. Cost saved: $200."

**ACTIONS:**
- Click "Analysis" tab
- Scroll through Business Impact dashboard
- Highlight:
  - Time Saved metric
  - Cost Saved metric
  - ROI percentage

---

### ‚è±Ô∏è Section 4: Impact & CTA (0:60-0:90) - 30 seconds

**VISUAL:** Show Business Impact dashboard, comparison table

**NARRATION:**
> "That's the power of multi-agent AI. What used to take 2 hours now takes 30 seconds. 85% accuracy versus 70% manual. Unlimited scalability. It's like having Iron Man's JARVIS for your infrastructure."

**PAUSE 2 seconds**

> "Try it now at [your-deployment-url] or check out the code on GitHub. Built for the hackathon with LangChain, LangGraph, and OpenAI."

**KEY POINTS:**
- Emphasize speed (2h ‚Üí 30s)
- Mention the "JARVIS" hook
- Give clear CTA (URL)
- End with tech stack for technical credibility

## üé• Recording Tips

### Before Recording

1. **Clean Your Desktop**
   - Close unnecessary apps
   - Hide distracting icons
   - Use a clean browser profile

2. **Prepare Your Browser**
   - Zoom to 100% or 125% (for readability)
   - Close extra tabs
   - Full screen mode (F11)

3. **Test Audio**
   - Use a decent microphone (laptop mic is OK)
   - Record 10 seconds to check levels
   - Speak clearly, moderate pace
   - Reduce background noise

4. **Practice the Script**
   - Run through 2-3 times without recording
   - Time yourself (aim for 60-75 seconds)
   - Mark where to pause for visual transitions

### During Recording

1. **Do Multiple Takes**
   - Record 3-5 full takes
   - Choose the best one
   - Don't worry about perfection

2. **Pacing**
   - Speak clearly, not too fast
   - Pause 1-2 seconds between sections
   - Let visuals breathe

3. **Smooth Actions**
   - Move mouse slowly
   - Use keyboard shortcuts when possible
   - Avoid erratic movements

4. **Timing the Progress**
   - The analysis takes ~30 seconds
   - Narrate while agents run
   - Don't skip the real-time visualization!

## ‚úÇÔ∏è Editing Tips (Optional)

### Basic Edits

1. **Trim Start/End**
   - Cut first 2-3 seconds (you clicking record)
   - Cut last 2-3 seconds (you stopping)

2. **Speed Up Slow Parts**
   - Speed up the 30-second analysis to 15-20 seconds
   - Keep narration at normal speed (voiceover)

3. **Add Text Overlays** (Optional but impactful)
   - "2 hours ‚Üí 30 seconds" 
   - "$200 saved per incident"
   - "6 AI agents working together"

4. **Add Music** (Very Optional)
   - Subtle background music
   - Low volume (10-20%)
   - Use royalty-free tracks

### Advanced Edits

1. **Picture-in-Picture**
   - Show your face in corner (builds trust)
   - Only if you're comfortable on camera

2. **Zoom Effects**
   - Zoom into important metrics
   - Highlight agent progress bars

3. **Transitions**
   - Simple fades between sections
   - Don't overdo it

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Show, Don't Tell**
   - Let the UI speak for itself
   - Less narration, more visuals

2. **Energy & Enthusiasm**
   - Sound excited (but not fake)
   - Smile while recording (it shows in voice!)

3. **First 10 Seconds Matter Most**
   - Hook judges immediately
   - If they're not interested by 10s, they'll skip

4. **Use the "JARVIS" Analogy**
   - It's memorable and relatable
   - Instantly communicates the concept

5. **Numbers, Numbers, Numbers**
   - Judges love quantifiable impact
   - Mention: 30s, 2h saved, $200, 6 agents, 85% accuracy

6. **End with Urgency**
   - "Try it now at..."
   - "Available for demo at..."
   - Make it easy for judges to test
